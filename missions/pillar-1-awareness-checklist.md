# Pillar 1 Awareness Checklist  
Part of the **AQ’S Corner Senior Cybersecurity Framework Model™**

Use this quick list anytime something pops up unexpectedly.

## ✔ Strange Time  
The message arrives late at night or very early.

## ✔ Unexpected Message  
You weren’t waiting for this bank, doctor, or company.

## ✔ Pressure or Urgency  
“Act now!” “Immediate!” “Don’t wait!”

## ✔ Something Doesn’t Match  
The story doesn’t line up with your real life.

---

## ⭐ What to Do
- Pause  
- Don’t click  
- Go directly to the website yourself  
- Ask someone you trust  

This simple awareness prevents most scams.
